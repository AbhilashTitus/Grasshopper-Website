About Us

Who Are We

We are Grasshopper Gastronomics, a Singapore based plant-based company. We create delicious, plant-based food that nourishes people, protects animals, and preserves the planet. Our products are free from animal ingredients, added oil, sugar, and synthetic additives crafted with care using high-quality ingredients from earth-loving farmers.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the food industry by making plant-based nutrition accessible to all, promoting health, sustainability, ethical living and and a greener planet.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing high-quality, nutritious, and ethical plant-based food that supports a healthier lifestyle and a greener planet. By valuing quality, health, and integrity, we aim to make sustainable eating the norm, not the exception.
